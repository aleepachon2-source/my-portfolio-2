The Challenge

Total Space Inc., a North Vancouver-based urban space optimization company, specializes in automated parking systems, retractable pool platforms, and smart city solutions. Despite having innovative technology and established operations, they lacked cohesive branding and digital infrastructure to effectively communicate their sophisticated engineering solutions to developers, architects, and municipalities. The company required:

  • A unified brand identity that reflected their innovative technology leadership
  • A modern website that could explain complex mechanical solutions clearly
  • Integrated CRM capabilities to manage B2B developer relationships
  • Video and paid media content to demonstrate their systems in action
  • Scalable marketing infrastructure for international expansion

The Solution

🎨

Comprehensive Brand Identity

Designed complete B2B brand system including logo redesign, brand guidelines, service catalogue, and promotional video showcasing automated parking and pool systems. Created cohesive visual language that communicates technical sophistication and innovation.

💻

Custom Website & CRM

Built and launched custom website with AI-driven chatbot and HubSpot CRM integration to manage developer inquiries and project pipelines. Designed all UI components and visual assets from concept to production, making complex engineering solutions accessible.

📹

Video-First Content Strategy

Produced video content demonstrating automated parking systems, retractable pools, and vehicle turntables in action. Created paid media assets for Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns targeting developers and architects. Maintained brand consistency across owned and paid channels.
